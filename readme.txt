== Description ==

Let reader and guest easily copy your embedded HTML URL to be shared into websites, IM Chats, forums, and other online publishing.

Once activated, it will make an additional form containing your embedded HTML snippets on the bottom of every posts. What's the function? If reader clicks it, the snippets will be copied and enable them to easily put URL on their blog, IMs, and Forums and let them promote your posts...

== Installation ==


1. Upload the zipped file to yoursite/wp-content/plugins
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
